NI Days is annual conference which gathers all engineers and companies interested in National Instruments products, mainly LabVIEW but also TestStand and so on.

This year I was there with my presentation about connecting ROS and LabVIEW. I shown my results of small project which was created just because we need to connect sbRIO board to ROS.

It was supprising for me, how many networking opportunities I had during this conference. I met Anna Kuzminski, who is responsible for introducing Linux to NI’s embedded systems. As she told me, the feture is bright, as NI have chosen Linux as their main system for embedded controllers (maybe also PXI?). This is especially interesting for me, as I like to use myRIO with C or C++.

Also, this looks that compiling ROS for myRIO seems to be more sensible than before.
